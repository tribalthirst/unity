Together We Rise:

Building Stronger Communities

Unity Infusion is a culturally-significant, grassroots organization on a mission to build socially connected, equitable, and empowered communities. We bridge divides, foster collaboration, and uplift marginalized voices – – one community at a time.

Take action now

Join the movement and become a part of our community.

At Unity Infusion, we believe that strong communities are built on genuine connections and shared purpose. Our vision is a world where every neighbor feels valued, heard, and empowered to co-create a thriving, equitable future. We do this by implementing a research-based, organizing  strategy that:

Intentionally seeks out and centers minoritized communities

Uplifts their voices

Facilitates connection WITHIN and BETWEEN these communities

Enables them to work ACROSS to the majority community, and

Fosters collaboration as they endeavor to co-lead their Beloved Community

Dr. JMarie Johnson-Kola, brings decades of experience championing social justice and equity, ensuring every community member has a seat at the table.
